Make Apps ready for ZOO 3.2

This tutorial will walk you through the process of making your custom app ready for ZOO 3.2.

There are only three steps needed to accomplish this task:

  • Create an 'edit' layout for your app. To do so just follow the steps described {doc: create-a-new-layout-with-positions text: here} or take a look at the ZOO standard apps.
  • Assign elements to your new 'edit' layout.
  • {doc: assign-elements-to-layout-positions text: Assign the Item Edit element to a layout of your choice}.

To enable comment frontend editing for your app just follow these steps:

If a template of your custom app overrides 'comments.php' you need to adjust 'comments.php' and copy '_edit.php'. You can simply copy these files from one of the ZOO standard apps.
